TurboTwig

Open-source procedural tree and vegetation generator for the browser — built with TypeScript and WebGL 2.0.

  • L-system grammars
  • WebGL 2.0 PBR rendering
  • Wind animation
  • Branch collision avoidance
  • glTF export
  • MIT open source

Procedural 3D trees in your browser: parametric L-systems, shadow mapping, atmospheric scattering, and glTF export for game engines.

  • Game development
  • Real-time visualization
  • Film & VFX previsualization
  • Research & education
  • Web 3D experiences
